The Initiation...

3/11/2020

1 Comment

 

Hey friends, as the title of my blog says, this blog is the archive of my present journey in the engineering universe. I know I am sort of exaggerating by adding the analogies but still I think it’s worth it. Moreover just like the expanding universe, the field of engineering is also expanding at a tremendous velocity. Every day engineers try to solve problems using their knowledge, intellect and experience. It is a well-accepted fact that engineers are one of the most important part this societal structure.

            Well I choose to pursue my career engineering field and currently pursuing my second year Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and communication (to be honest I am writing this blog because we are blessed with the semester holidays. Oh thank you lord, gracious). 
​                     
                     My engineering journey began first began virtually in my imagination paradox in the most complex organ of the human namely brain. Yeah, I have I brain too. When I entered my Pre-University college I got to witness a future planning workshop (for students) in which we were given very crucial intel regarding the various fields we could choose to lead the ship of life. Well as they began to unwrap the chest of engineering knowledge it caught my attention (this was surprising cause that day I realized that I had the ability to pay attention). I had I bad habit of day-dreaming and imagining which surely took place at that instant. I thought this is my thing and was getting Goosebumps. Well that’s just the virtual start of my experience with engineering.
                 
                During this period I dreamt of joining IIT. Well four years passed it is still my dream. I learnt that only those who really deserve and really hard working and worked honestly other than just dreaming are the ones who are worthy of being the part of IIT.
