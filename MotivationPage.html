Motivational Quotes


Here are some great motivational words which inspired me. May these words be  inspiration to all my readers too.


"I have been impressed with the urgency of doing. Knowing is not enough; we must apply. Being willing is not enough; we must do.” 
         – Leonardo da Vinci


 “If I had nine hours to chop down a tree, I’d spend the first six sharpening my axe.”
                                                                                                                – Abraham Lincoln


" Scientists investigate that which already is; Engineers create that which has never been."
                                                                                                              - Albert Einstein

"No one person can shape the life of another. Your success and happiness depend upon your own self. Think for yourself and have a plan of life."
                                                                                                             - Sir M Visvesvaraya


“What we usually consider as impossible are simply engineering problems… there’s no law of physics preventing them.”
                                                                                                              - Michio Kaku


“One man’s “magic” is another man’s engineering. “Supernatural” is a null word."
                                                                                                           – Robert A. Heinlein

“As engineers, we were going to be in a position to change the world – not just study it.”
                                                                                                        - Henry Petroski


“Scientists study the world as it is; engineers create the world that has never been.”
                                                                                                        -Theodore von Karman
